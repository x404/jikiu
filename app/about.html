About

"JIKIU" is a Japanese word that translates as strength, or endurance, the qualities demonstrated by the excellent reliability and quality of spare pads in more than 60 countries around the world.

Japanese spare parts from the JIKIU are quality products at an affordable price. The entire range is shipped from the RMP NAGAMOCHI Technology Japan Co.. Ltd. All parts are manufactured under the strict supervision of the company's Japanese laboratory.
